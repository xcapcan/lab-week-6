What is a web server? Why would you need one?

A Web server is a program that uses HTTP (Hypertext Transfer Protocol) to serve the files that form Web pages to users, in response to their requests, which are forwarded by their computers' HTTP clients. You would need a web server is to store your website’s files to broadcast to other users.

Explain why getting a domain name is not enough to put your site up on the web.

Because even though you have a domain name, it doesn’t mean that your website is now being broadcasted, because you need to register with a web hosting service to make it live

What service does a website hosting company provide?

It provides service and technology to live broadcast your website with your domain live on the web.

What are the most important characteristics to look for in selecting a good web hosting company?

You need to have in mind how much hand holding you’ll need and the amount of traffic you expect, when you’re choosing the web hosting company and see which one accommodates your needs.

Research companies that provide website hosting plans. On average, what is the price range (price per month) for hosting a website?

Usually $3-$10 per month. If VIP hosting, it can range from $30 to $50 per month. It depends on the type of hosting, because there are many.

What are the most important features for you when reviewing a hosting plan?

The nature of your website, the cost, shared vs. dedicated hosting, reputation and reviews of the host, addon domain services,the speed of your host and user-friendliness.
